6.2b Class Activity: Numeric Expressions and the Distributive Property

Activity 1:

a. Luca is buying practice uniforms for soccer.

Each jersey costs $20 and each pair of shorts costs $30.

He purchases two practice jerseys and two pairs of practice shorts.

Write an expression that can be used to represent the total amount Luca spends on practice uniforms.

Then, simplify the expression to show the total amount Luca spends on practice uniforms.
