Activity 2: Algebraic Expressions

In this activity, you will re-visit some of the problems from 6.1d and examine the different algebraic expressions that were written to represent the problems. While doing this, think about the following questions:

  1. What does it mean to say that two algebraic expressions are equivalent? How can you show two algebraic expressions are equivalent?
  2. What does it mean to simplify an algebraic expression?
